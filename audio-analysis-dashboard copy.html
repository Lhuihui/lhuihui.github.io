Audio Analysis

Audio Analysis app is one of Watson starter-kit apps which is aimed to demonstrate a use case using multiple Watson cognitive services/APIs. In this project, I led the design team coming up an engaging design solution that brings out the business value of concept insights and speech services in a way consumable by general population and inspirational in dev community. I built trust-based partnership with offering management and engineering leaders by organizing design playbacks leading to several design iterations. Organized daily scrum with dev teams to break down the tasks, contribute to the issues myself as front-end and track other dev's issues to promise the projects delivering on time with high design and engineering standard.
