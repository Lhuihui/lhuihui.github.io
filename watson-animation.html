Connect Watson brand with galaxy
Overview
In september 2016, Watson launched a new set of brand value - Essential, Meticulous, Alive, Wonderful. And we think there is a potential to use galaxy as a metaphor to illustrate Watson brand in animation.
We did a fun field trip to NASA to experience lunar, martian and micro-gravity, and translate the experience to animation concept.
My role
Motion designer
Why galaxy
Galaxy inspires wonder, awe, and curiosity
In a galaxy, particles are related to each other by attraction and repulsion through gravity. In a world of knowledge, Watson finds relationships within large bodies of information.
