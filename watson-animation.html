Overview
In september 2016, Watson launched a new set of brand value - Essential, Alive, Wonderful. And we think there is a potential to use galaxy as a metaphor to illustrate Watson brand in animation.
Why galaxy
Galaxy inspires wonder, awe, and curiosity

We did a fun field trip to NASA to experience lunar, martian and micro-gravity, and translate the experience to animation concept.
