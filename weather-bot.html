Conversation Bot Starter Kit

Overview
Conversation Bot Starter Kit combines Watson conversation services and weather company APIs, integrating with Twilio and Facebook, helping developers get started with Watson conversation framework in multiple channel like SMS and iMessage.
My task is to design a weather bot experience which could be deployed in multiple platforms and served as an education materials for developers to learn about best practice of conversation design.
Design
Conversation Logic Design
For developer education, there are several aspects I'm trying to incorporate in the conversation logic design:
  • Conversation flow follows intent.
  • Off topic handling.
  • When to use close list entities VS use Alchemy language to detect entities.
  • Disambiguate entites.
    • Voice of Tone
    Watson can be a know-it-all without knowing it. While we are confident in Watson's tremendous capabilities, we should seek to offset his bluntness by never sounding arrogant or brash in our communications. Here are some tips I'm using to create a conversational, humanizing tone that people can connect with:
  • Speak in complete sentences.
  • Create a conversation.
  • Use natural language -- avoid hyperbole, jargon and slang.
  • Provoke with unknown, useful insights.
  • Know your audience; speak their language.
    • Developer Education
    Besides conversation with the bot, I'm trying to reveal the conversation logic in the UI, and at the end of the chat, provide developers the path to the documentation.
