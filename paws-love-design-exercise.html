Field Trip (2.5 hours)
After having the big picture of pet adoption ecosystem, I think shelter is a key place to understand current pet matching experience, so I decided to have a field trip in Texas Human Heroes.
Seeing so many cute pets waiting in the cage, I developed a deep empathy for them. Most of them are very lonely with longing for a companion, geniune care and freedom.
I had some interesting findings about how shelters were delivering information to potential adopters. The pet information was in the paper hanging on the animal cage. The shelter staff told me most of the pet age, breeds information is based their guess.
They are also hanging a lof educational information about how to read animals, how they would categorize animals based on how easy they can be handled.

Define project goals (0.5 hours)
After understanding the pet adoption system, and hearing my friends' stories about how they used to make decisions and how that effected the outcome of their pet relationship. I feel like the key problem is there is not enough data about the pet for adopters to make profound decisions.
Based on the shelter staff, their animals are from all over the place. When they got the animals, they didn't know their histories. They can only rely on the staff/volunteers' personal experience with the animals to help adopters to match the right one for them. Unfortunately, volunteers come and go. Most of the animal information didn't get fully transferred.
So I think one big part of the solution should be providing an easy pet information documenting system to collect as much as data about the pet. Another big part of the solution is to provide adopters a cognitive personalized pet content platform for pet discovery, matching, recommendation and pet raising education.
From user perspective, I defined three MVP "needs statement":
  • Pet adopters need a way to get a better feel for the animals and have trustworthy information so that they can choose the pet that is the right fit for them.
  • Pet adopters need a way to learn pet care expertise so that they feel prepared instead of anxious.
  • Volunteers need an easier, more effective way to share their expertise and provide guidance to potential adopters.
    • And one nice to have "needs statement":
  • Shelter staff needs a way to track adopters' behave after adoption so that they can recommend future pets to good record adopters and help animals when they detect bad adopters.
    • Design for volunteers (2 hours)
    Volunteers need an easier, more effective way to share their expertise and provide guidance to potential adopters.
    Pet info content problem
    Based on the research, current pet information is provided in text format, which is very hard to visualize. So in the design, I'm trying to encourage voluteers/foster families to create pet story in the form of photos and videos as solution.
    Decide solution platform
    Considering the environment when volunteers, shelter staff, foster familites are interacting the pets, their mobile phone probably will be the most accessible device at that moment. And it's better to be able to get access to camera, so I decide to design a mobile native app which will help volunteers to co-create pet story videos..
    Entry point
    To avoid lengthy pet profile retrieving process, I'm thinking letting volunteers scan a QR code on each pet's cage to quickly retrieve pet profile.

    1s frame creation will need more creative thoughts from volunteers to choose the most presentative moment to add to the pet storyline.

    Tags and comments
    The tags and comments will surface on pet searching website as a useful reference information for adopters.
    The tag information can be used to train the matching algorithm in the system.
    Volunteer story and pet tracking
    Volunteers and foster familites are selflessly contributing their time helping the animals.
    Providing their volunteer story video could be served as a form of rewards. And the system will help them track the pet they helped and where those pets go.
    Design for pet adopters (2.5 hours)
    Pet adopters need a way to get a better feel for the animals and have trustworthy information so that they can choose the pet that is the right fit for them.
    Pet adopters need a way to learn pet care expertise so that they feel prepared instead of anxious.
    Decide solution platform
    Considering most of people will explore pet information online at their computer, I decided to design a website which provides personalized contents for pet adopters to easily discover and match available pets, and get pet raising education..
    Paws Love website flow and highlights
    For this site visual design, I chose the light scheme instead of the dark scheme used in the mobile app design. I kept purple as dominate branding color and used the same typography and UI elements like rounded buttons to keep the style consistent.
    Homepage
    The homepage will have a large range of audience. I hope to provide them a fun casual browsering experience, so we can attract the newbies coming to the community, and keep the community active.
    About live stream
    One type of content I was trying to provide here is live stream. My idea came from the fied trip to texas human hero. I met several girls and they were there playing with a lot of animals and they seem very experienced. They told me they were not looking for a pet, but they like animals. They generally spent a lot of weekends to check out animals.
    I felt like for those pet enthusiast, in shelter, we can set up a video recording environment for them. They can live stream or record how they interact with animals, which can be served as part of the educational materials.

    Pet profile page
    The pet profile page will provide the pet story video, volunteers' tagging and comments content, finanacial cost estimate, and a match analysis to help user evaluate the pets.
    Personalize content
    The system is tracking users' clicks and followed pets. The more it learns about users' interest, the more personalized contents it can provide for them.
    Cognitive matching
    Based on the pet match information user was provided when signing up, the system can generate matching analysis hypothesis. And it will take users' feedback to improve the matching accuracy.
